How it works

Your own private automation environment, ready in 2 minutes

Suretek runs a secure agent on your local network so your data never has to leave your building.

1

Sign up

Create your account and get your own private scheduler environment instantly — no waiting, no setup calls.

2

Install the agent

Download the lightweight local agent from your dashboard and run it on any Windows or Linux machine inside your network. Takes under 2 minutes.

3

Create your jobs

Relax

Suretek runs your jobs on schedule and logs every result. You'll know immediately if something fails.
